AskNanterre

What is it ?

An android mobile application to make interactions between students and the professor at university easier.

Download
Source Code

Features

Understand their level

As the professor, adapt your class according to questions asked by the students

Answer questions

As the professor, answer to students' questions directly on the application at any time

Interrogate students

As the professor, make quizs for the students and follow their progress

Answer professor's quizs

As a student, train and improve yourself by answering to professor's quizs

Ask questions to professor

As a student, ask questions directly to professor without disturbing the class

Intriging questions

As a student, get a list of questions selected by the professor.
